Our Story

Baking joy since day one

Essy Baker was born from a simple belief: that a truly great cake can transform any gathering into a memory. We combine time-honoured baking techniques with contemporary design to produce cakes that taste as beautiful as they look.

From intimate birthday celebrations to grand wedding receptions, every order is treated with the same meticulous care. We source premium ingredients, bake fresh to order, and deliver with a smile.

  • 100% fresh, premium ingredients — no preservatives
  • Fully bespoke designs tailored to your vision
  • Flexible dietary options: gluten-free, dairy-free & vegan
  • Same-day delivery available within our service area
